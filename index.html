About Alfonso's

Alfonso's Shell Shack is a seafood restaurant located in the heart of a coastal town known for its fresh catch and oceanfront charm. Established in 1985, Alfonso's has been a beloved institution in the community, serving up delicious seafood dishes that keep locals and visitors alike coming back for more.

At Alfonso's, the menu features a wide variety of seafood dishes, including classic favorites like clam chowder, fish and chips, and shrimp scampi, as well as more unique offerings like lobster mac and cheese and grilled octopus. The restaurant also boasts an extensive wine list and a rotating selection of local craft beers.

The atmosphere at Alfonso's is warm and welcoming, with a nautical theme that celebrates the restaurant's coastal location. The dining room is cozy and intimate, perfect for a romantic dinner for two or a family celebration. In the summertime, diners can enjoy their meals on the outdoor patio, taking in the fresh sea air and the sounds of the nearby waves.

At Alfonso's Shell Shack, the focus is on fresh, quality seafood, prepared with care and served with a smile. Whether you're a seafood aficionado or a first-time visitor, Alfonso's is the perfect place to experience the best that the ocean has to offer.
